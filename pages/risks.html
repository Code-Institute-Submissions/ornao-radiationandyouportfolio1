Are there any risks associated with radiation?


It is known that exposure to very high levels of radiation can cause cancer.



However, it is unknown for certain, if there is any risk associated with exposure to x-rays in Radiology departments. If a risk exists at all, it is very low.



Consequently, we always strive to use the smallest number of x-rays to acquire an image that can answer your doctor’s question.



It is important to understand that the risk of NOT having the x-ray exam may be much greater than the small risk associated with the procedure itself.



In other words, the benefit associated with the x-ray exam exceeds the small risk.
